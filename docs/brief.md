# iKITES Website — Product Brief

> Captured from the founding brief. Source of truth for positioning, audience, and tone.

## What we are

iKITES is an **AI-first software engineering company**. Being AI-first is concrete, not a slogan:
all of our software processes are aligned with AI. We run **swarms of agents** to make work faster
and more productive, and we **continuously experiment with AI** to keep delivery cost-efficient for
our customers.

## Target segments

### Startups — speed and rapid product development
- **Early-stage startups:** focus on speed and "small-ticket" items that accelerate their
  go-to-market. They bring the idea; our engineering team brings it to life quickly.
- **Established startups:** continued speed as the product and team scale.

### Enterprises — speed applied to complexity
- Focus is also speed, but specifically around **re-engineering** existing systems and
  **managing complexity**.

## Technology verticals
1. **Agentic AI**
2. **Computer Vision**
3. **Embedded Systems**

## Tone of voice
- **Well-grounded, not overtly ambitious.**
- If the website were a person: **confident, but not brazen.** Not flaunting.
- Communicate what we do in a **measured, balanced** way — neither overconfident nor underconfident.
- **Every word should earn its place** and speak directly to the right customer.
